About Me

My name is James White, and I am a computer science graduate from UNC Charlotte. As an avid fan of both NBA basketball and technology, I combined my passion for sports and my academic background to create this website.

I developed this platform to showcase the skills and knowledge I acquired during my time at UNC Charlotte. It's not just a project; it's a testament to my dedication and enthusiasm for data science and web development.

With this website, I aim to provide NBA enthusiasts, data lovers, and anyone curious about basketball with a place to explore and compare player statistics in an interactive and informative manner. I hope you enjoy using the tools and resources I've put together.
